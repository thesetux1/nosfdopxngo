About Us

We are a non-profit NGO supporting individuals with financial grants and job placement opportunities. We aim to empower communities across Nigeria by offering ₦300,000 support grants and connecting qualified applicants to job opportunities.

300,000 - 600,000 Grant Information

What is the Grant?

The ₦300,000 Empowerment Grant is designed to support small business owners, students, and unemployed youth across Nigeria. This fund can be used to start or grow a business, pay school fees, or develop career skills.

Who Can Apply?

  • Nigerians aged 16 and above
  • Must have a valid phone number
  • Must provide accurate information

How to Apply
